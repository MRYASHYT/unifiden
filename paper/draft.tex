\begin{abstract}
Agentic AI pipelines fail silently and systematically in production, yet no standardized framework exists to detect or classify these failures. This paper introduces AgentStress, an industrial-grade reliability testing framework. We propose a methodology for conducting controlled experiments across instruction clarity levels to identify distinct failure modes and investigate hallucination propagation in multi-agent networks.
\end{abstract}

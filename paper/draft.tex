\begin{abstract}
Agentic AI pipelines fail silently and systematically in production, yet no standardized framework exists to detect or classify these failures. This paper introduces AgentStress, an industrial-grade reliability testing framework. We conduct 300 controlled experiments across three instruction clarity levels. Our results identify 7 distinct failure modes and demonstrate how multi-agent networks propagate hallucinations.
\end{abstract}
